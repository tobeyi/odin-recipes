Air Fryer Chicken Legs

Descripion

Dive into the simplicity of making scrumptious Air Fryer Chicken Legs with us! This recipe is your golden ticket to a fuss-free meal that doesn't skimp on flavor. Perfect for busy weeknights or when you're craving something delicious without the wait, these chicken legs come out perfectly crispy on the outside and tender on the inside every time. With just a few ingredients and your trusty air fryer, you're minutes away from a meal that's sure to impress. Let's get started and turn those ordinary chicken legs into a extraordinary dinner treat!

Ingredients

  • Chicken Legs - 4 to 6
  • Olive Oil - 2 tablespoons
  • Garlic Powder - 1 teaspoon
  • Paprika - 1 teaspoon
  • Salt - ½ teaspoon
  • Black Pepper - ¼ teaspoon

Steps

  1. Preheat the Air Fryer: Set your air fryer to 380°F (193°C) and let it warm up for about 3-5 minutes. This ensures even cooking right from the start.
  2. Prepare the Chicken: Pat the chicken legs dry with paper towels. This helps the skin get crispy.
  3. Season: In a small bowl, mix together the olive oil, garlic powder, paprika, salt, and black pepper. Rub this mixture all over the chicken legs, making sure they're well-coated.
  4. Arrange in Air Fryer: Place the chicken legs in the air fryer basket in a single layer. Make sure they're not touching so the hot air can circulate freely, ensuring even cooking and crispiness.
  5. Cook: Air fry the chicken legs for about 25-30 minutes. Halfway through the cooking time, flip the legs to ensure they brown and crisp up evenly on all sides.
  6. Check for Doneness: The chicken is done when it reaches an internal temperature of 165°F (74°C) at the thickest part of the leg, away from the bone. Use a meat thermometer to check.
  7. Rest and Serve: Let the chicken legs rest for a few minutes after cooking. This allows the juices to redistribute, keeping the meat moist. Serve hot and enjoy!
