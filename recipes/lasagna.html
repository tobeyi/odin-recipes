Lasagna

Description

Hello there, today we will be discussing one of my most favorite dishes, Lasagna! This dish is a true comfort food classic, perfect for any day that calls for something warm, cheesy, and utterly delicious. Lasagna is like a hug in the form of food, with its layers of soft pasta, rich sauce, and creamy cheese all coming together to create a masterpiece that's sure to impress anyone who takes a bite.

On this page, I will show you a simple way to make this delicious meal. I hope you like it just as much as me and my family does!

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles - 9 to 12 sheets
  • Ricotta cheest - 15 ounces
  • Large egg
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese - 2 cups
  • Grated Parmesan cheese - half a cup
  • Marinara sauce - 24 ounces
  • Ground beef - 1 pound
  • Salt and pepper - to taste
  • Fresh basil
