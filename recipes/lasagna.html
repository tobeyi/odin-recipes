Lasagna

Description

Hello there, today we will be discussing one of my most favorite dishes, Lasagna! This dish is a true comfort food classic, perfect for any day that calls for something warm, cheesy, and utterly delicious. Lasagna is like a hug in the form of food, with its layers of soft pasta, rich sauce, and creamy cheese all coming together to create a masterpiece that's sure to impress anyone who takes a bite.

On this page, I will show you a simple way to make this delicious meal. I hope you like it just as much as me and my family does!

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles - 9 to 12 sheets
  • Ricotta cheest - 15 ounces
  • Large egg
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese - 2 cups
  • Grated Parmesan cheese - half a cup
  • Marinara sauce - 24 ounces
  • Ground beef - 1 pound
  • Salt and pepper - to taste
  • Fresh basil

Steps

  1. Preheat Oven: Set your oven to 375°F (190°C) to ensure it's ready for baking.
  2. Cook Pasta: Boil the penne pasta in salted water until it's just al dente, usually about 1 minute less than the package instructions. Drain well.
  3. Brown Ground Beef: While the pasta cooks, brown the ground beef in a large skillet over medium heat. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Once fully cooked, drain any excess fat.
  4. Mix Marinara: Stir the marinara sauce into the cooked ground beef, combining them thoroughly.
  5. Prepare Cheese Mixture: In a bowl, mix the ricotta cheese with the egg, and season with a little salt and pepper. This mixture adds richness and helps bind the ingredients.
  6. Combine Ingredients: In a large mixing bowl, gently combine the cooked pasta with the beef and marinara sauce mixture. Fold in the ricotta mixture and half of the shredded mozzarella cheese until everything is evenly distributed.
  7. Assemble in Baking Dish: Transfer the mixture to a 9x13 inch baking dish. Sprinkle the remaining mozzarella and all of the grated Parmesan cheese evenly over the top.
  8. Bake: Cover the dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes. Then, remove the foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until the cheese on top is golden and bubbly.
  9. Rest and Serve: Let the "lazy lasagna" rest for a few minutes after baking. This makes it easier to serve and allows the flavors to meld beautifully.
