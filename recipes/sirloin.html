Sirloin Steak

Ingredients

  • Sirloin Steaks - 2 steaks, about 1 inch thick
  • Salt - to taste, preferably coarse or kosher for better seasoning
  • Black Pepper - freshly ground, to taste
  • Olive Oil - 2 tablespoons, for searing
  • Butter - 2 tablespoons, for basting and added flavor
  • Garlic - 2 cloves, lightly crushed (optional)
  • Fresh Rosemary or Thyme - a few sprigs (optional), for aromatic infusion
